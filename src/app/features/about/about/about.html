How FarmWise AI came to be

Founded in 2020 by a team of agricultural scientists and AI researchers, FarmWise AI emerged from a shared vision to bridge the gap between traditional farming wisdom and modern technology. Our founders recognized that while farmers possess invaluable experience, they often lack access to the data-driven insights that could significantly improve their outcomes.

Starting with a small pilot program in Punjab, we've grown to serve thousands of farmers across India. Our AI models, trained on vast datasets of soil conditions, weather patterns, and crop performance, now provide accurate yield predictions and personalized farming recommendations to help farmers optimize their operations.

Today, we continue to innovate, constantly improving our algorithms and expanding our services to meet the evolving needs of the agricultural community. Our commitment remains unchanged: to empower every farmer with the tools they need to succeed in an increasingly complex agricultural landscape.
