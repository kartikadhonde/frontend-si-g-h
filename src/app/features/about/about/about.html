Project Overview

AI-Powered Crop Yield Prediction and Optimization

This project addresses the critical need for data-driven agricultural insights in India. Developed for the Smart India Hackathon 2025 under the Agriculture, FoodTech & Rural Development theme, our solution leverages machine learning models to predict crop yields using historical agricultural data, weather patterns, and soil health metrics.

Our XGBoost-based model processes data from 54 different crops across 30 Indian states, utilizing target encoding for crops and states, plus one-hot encoding for 6 distinct seasons. The model is trained with 2000 estimators, a learning rate of 0.3, and early stopping at 50 rounds to prevent overfitting and ensure optimal performance.

The model analyzes key agricultural factors including crop area, annual rainfall, fertilizer usage, pesticide application, and seasonal patterns to predict crop yield. The platform provides actionable recommendations for irrigation, fertilization, and pest control, tailored to specific crops and regional conditions. With support for regional languages and a focus on small-scale farmers, we aim to increase agricultural productivity by at least 10% through intelligent, data-driven farming practices.
